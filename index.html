Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

In the heart of our community, eMobilis began with a simple yet profound vision: to create an educational environment where young minds could flourish beyond the constraints of traditional learning. What started as a modest initiative by a group of passionate educators in 1985 has blossomed into one of the region's most respected institutions for innovative education.

1965

eMobilis was founded. The school began with just 45 students in a converted Victorian mansion.

1982

After years of steady growth, eMobilis expanded to its current main campus, introducing innovative science laboratories and one of the region's first computer learning centers. The school's commitment to technology-enhanced learning became a cornerstone of its identity.

1998

eMobilis launched its internationally recognized Integrated Arts & Sciences Program, breaking down traditional subject barriers. This pioneering approach to interdisciplinary education attracted attention from educational researchers worldwide and became a model for schools across the country.

2010

The school completed its state-of-the-art Innovation Center, featuring flexible learning spaces, advanced digital media studios, and collaborative workspaces. This facility embodied Egadds' commitment to preparing students for the challenges of a rapidly evolving global landscape.
